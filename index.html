Bank Campaign Analysis

Introduction

The data chosen for this visualization analysis came from a direct marketing effort run by a Australian bank. The main way people were contacted was through phone calls. The goal of the effort was to get people to sign up for bank term deposits. The main goal is to use visualizations to find out what kind of people signed up for the term deposit and which months of the campaign were the most popular.
